Desserts

Description

Dessert, a sweet course typically served at the end of a meal, comes in various forms ranging from cakes and pastries to custards and ice creams. In Pakistan, desserts are often infused with rich flavors, aromatic spices, and sometimes even vibrant colors. Here are ten popular types of desserts in Pakistan
  1. Gulab Jamun
  2. Jalebi
  3. Ras Malai
  4. Barfi
  5. Kheer
  6. Sheer Khurma
  7. Falooda
  8. Mithai
  9. Rabri
  10. Sohan Halwa
