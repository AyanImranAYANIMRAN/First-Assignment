Burger

Description

A burger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked patty, typically made from ground meat, placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. It often includes additional ingredients such as cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, bacon, or condiments like ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, and relish. Burgers are a staple in many cuisines worldwide and can be customized in numerous ways to suit different tastes.
