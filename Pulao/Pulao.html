Pulao

Description

Pulao, also spelled Pilaf or Pilau, is a popular dish in Pakistani cuisine known for its aromatic rice cooked with spices and often combined with vegetables, meats, or both. It's distinct from biryani in that pulao usually involves cooking the rice together with the ingredients, while biryani involves cooking the rice and meat separately and then layering them together.
