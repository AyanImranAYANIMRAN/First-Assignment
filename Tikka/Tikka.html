Tikka

Description

Tikka is a popular South Asian dish, particularly famous in Pakistan, consisting of marinated pieces of meat (commonly chicken or beef) that are grilled or roasted. The word "Tikka" means "bits" or "pieces." The meat is typically marinated with a mixture of spices and yogurt to enhance flavor and tenderness. It is then cooked on skewers, often over a charcoal grill, which gives it a distinct smoky flavor.
