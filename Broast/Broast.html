Broast

Description

Broast is a popular dish in Pakistan, originating from the Middle Eastern and American styles of fried chicken. It involves marinating chicken pieces, typically with a blend of spices, and then frying them using a pressure fryer, which helps to retain the moisture and flavor of the chicken while making the exterior crispy. The result is juicy, flavorful chicken with a crispy outer layer.
