Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is a sweet, frozen dessert typically made from dairy products, such as milk and cream, combined with sweeteners and flavorings. This mixture is churned and cooled to incorporate air and prevent large ice crystals from forming, resulting in a smooth and creamy texture. Ice cream can be enjoyed in various forms, including cones, cups, sundaes, and milkshakes, and can be customized with a wide array of flavors and toppings.
