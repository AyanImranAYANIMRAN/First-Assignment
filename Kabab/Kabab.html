Kabab

Description

Kabab (also spelled kebab) refers to a variety of grilled or skewered meat dishes originating from the Middle East and South Asia. In Pakistani cuisine, kababs are typically made from ground or minced meat, including beef, mutton, chicken, or fish, mixed with a variety of spices and herbs. The mixture is then shaped and cooked on a grill or in an oven. Kababs are often served with naan, rice, or as part of a larger meal, accompanied by chutneys, raita, and salads.
